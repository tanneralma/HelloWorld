Fantasy Kids

Building character, through building characters.

Development and discovery

Fantasy kids is a summer gathering experience specifically for ages 9 -13. Fantasy kids encourages discovery, imagination and creativity from it's youth participants. Youth will get the opportunity to step out of their comfort zone by acting as their chosen character on a guided quest, making and strengthening friendships, developing communication skills, and learning medieval customs including safe sword play. Fantasy kids comes to you! Our services can be offered anywhere in Utah county, including but not limited to public parks or personal residences. We are ready to customize the experience to any time frame, location or group size. Fantasy Kids charges $15 per person for a four hour session, however there are also group deals offered on the events page.


Vocabulary

  • RPG (role playing game) - "A game in which players assume the roles of characters in a fictional setting. Players take responsibility for acting out these roles within a narrative, either through literal acting, or through a process of structured decision-making and regarding character development."

  • LARP (live-action role-playing) - "A type of interactive role-playing game in which the participants portray characters through physical action, often in costume and with props."



    • Campaign, create, clash.

    The typical session will have a 3 hour long tabletop RPG. Participants must communicate well and work together to finish the campaign. There will be several breaks where youth will do some medieval style art, medieval board games, folk lore, and dancing. For the last hour, youth get play with foam swords, water blasters, and cardboard shields for an epic battle to the end. Foam swords will be provided, as well as rules and safety instructions to keep the experience safe and friendly.
