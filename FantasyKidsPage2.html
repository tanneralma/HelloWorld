How many people are involved with fantasy kids?

Fantasy Kids is Currently operated solely by 1 person, Tanner Heaton. If there are enough requests for the camp, more DMs will be recruited.


Can Fantasy kids offer services beyond the boundaries of Utah county?

Yes, but there may be extra payment needed for transportation difficulties depending on where the session is desired to take place. This extra payment may be discussed through email or over phone


How can customers assure the professionality of the Fantasy Kids system?

Fantasy kids assures there will be no swearing, physical intervening, or innapropriate language of any kind. Fantasy kids is a family friendly expirience.


What should my youth bring to Fantasy Kids?

Make sure that your youth has a lunch with him, or has eaten before the camp. Clothing that is comfortable and durable are recommended for the experience as well.


What are your cancellation, transfer, substitution, and refund policies?

If payment for the camp is made, we cannot refund the transfer. If a session is cancelled, you may rescheduel within the next week for the small fee of 10$. We can also substitute your session payment toward another youth or family to enjoy the session in their place.
